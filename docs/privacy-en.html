Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Bucket ("the App") values your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal information.

  • No login or account creation is required.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not collect location data.

2. Data Storage

All data created within the App (bucket lists, goals, progress) is stored locally on your device only.

  • No data is transmitted to external servers.
  • No data is shared with third parties.
  • Deleting the App removes all data from your device.

3. Analytics and Advertising

  • We do not use any analytics or tracking tools.
  • We do not display advertisements.
  • We do not collect advertising identifiers.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be communicated through app updates.
