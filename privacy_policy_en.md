# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 13, 2026**

---

## Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how **Bucket List** ("the App") handles user information.

---

## Data Collection

**This App does not collect any personal information.**

- No account creation or login is required.
- No personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number) is collected.
- No location data is collected.
- No device information (photos, contacts) is accessed.

---

## Data Storage

All data created within the App (bucket lists, goals, progress) is stored **locally on your device only**.

- No data is transmitted to external servers.
- No data is shared with third parties.
- Deleting the App will remove all data from your device.

---

## Analytics and Advertising

- No analytics or tracking tools are used.
- No advertisements are displayed.
- No advertising identifiers are collected.

---

## Third-Party Sharing

This App does not share any user data with third parties.

---

## Children's Privacy

This App does not intentionally collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
